Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website? +
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use? +
Yes, most of our content is completely free. Some premium features and advanced courses may require a subscription.
3. Do I need to create an account? +
No, you can browse and use basic features without an account. However, creating an account allows you to track your progress and save your learning history.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary? +
We provide daily vocabulary lessons, flashcards, word games, and contextual usage examples to help you learn and remember new words effectively.
